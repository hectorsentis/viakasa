Compact label for status (En venta, Vendido) or category (Obra nueva, Lujo). Uses the brand caps face.

```jsx
<Badge variant="gold" tone="solid">Destacado</Badge>
<Badge variant="success">En venta</Badge>
<Badge variant="navy" tone="line">Obra nueva</Badge>
```

Variants: `gold | navy | neutral | success | warning | danger`. Tones: `solid | soft | line`.
